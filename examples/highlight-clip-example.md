# Highlight Clip Example

## Source

- Source type: livestream or interview
- Goal: 30-second highlight
- Angle: one memorable insight

## Raw Segment

The speaker takes 20 seconds to get to the point, adds background, then finally gives the useful sentence near the end.

## Edited Version

1. Start from the strongest sentence
2. Add one short line of setup if needed
3. End immediately after the payoff

## Example Shape

- Hook: "Most creators do not need more footage. They need better clip selection."
- Body: one short explanation
- End: one memorable conclusion

## Why This Works

- the opening is direct
- the clip has one idea
- the ending lands cleanly
