# Video Highlight Checklist

Use this checklist before exporting or publishing a highlight clip.

## Clip Selection

- Is there a clear emotional or informational peak?
- Would a new viewer understand why this moment matters?
- Is the clip built around one main payoff?

## Opening

- Does the clip get to the point quickly?
- Can the first 1 to 3 seconds be stronger?
- Did you remove filler, greetings, or weak setup?

## Structure

- Is the clip easy to follow without extra context?
- Does every shot or line support the same idea?
- Is anything repetitive or slow?

## Packaging

- Is the title clear and specific?
- Are subtitles readable on mobile?
- Does the on-screen text support the main point?

## Ending

- Does the clip end cleanly?
- Is the final beat satisfying or intentional?
- If the goal is discovery, is there a reason to watch more?

## Platform Fit

- Is the clip shaped for Shorts, Reels, TikTok, or another channel?
- Does the pacing match the destination platform?
- Would a different version perform better on another platform?

## Quality Control

- Audio balanced
- Captions accurate
- Visual framing checked
- No dead air
- No confusing transitions

## Final Test

If you watched this clip without seeing the source, would it still feel complete, interesting, and worth finishing?
